Featured Project

Solar Mesh

Solar Mesh is a low-cost monitoring and communications system for small off-grid solar systems. It is designed to help users and organizations track panel input, battery status, and load output, while making silent failures easier to detect.

Problem

Small solar systems often fail silently, leaving users without clear diagnostics.

Solution

A retrofit monitoring node that measures power data and communicates wirelessly.

Impact

Better visibility, longer battery life, faster maintenance, and improved trust in solar systems.
