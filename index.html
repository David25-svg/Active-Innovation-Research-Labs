Flagship Research

Solar Mesh

Solar Mesh is AIRL’s first major research program: a low-cost monitoring and communication platform for small-scale off-grid solar systems.

The Problem

Many small solar systems fail silently, leaving users and technicians without clear information about panel input, battery health, load output, or system failures.

The Approach

Solar Mesh explores field-deployable monitoring nodes, wireless communication, and remote diagnostics to make system health visible and actionable.

The Impact

Better reliability, longer system life, improved maintenance decisions, and greater trust in distributed energy systems.
